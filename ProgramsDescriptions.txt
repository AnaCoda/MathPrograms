Partitions.py:
Find all possible lists of numbers which add up to n

PermsAndCombs.py:
Print the permutations or combinations of a range of numbers. Easily reused if you would like to use a specific list.

Factorials.py:
Prints the factorial of a number.

QuadraticEquation.py:
Solves quadratic functions with the quadratic equation. It will tell you how many solutions there are.

MeanMedianMode.py:
Tell you the mean, median, and mode of a set of data. It will also sort it.

PrimeFactorization.py:
Will print the prime factorization of a number.

ChickenMcNugget.py:
Tells you the greatest integer that cannot be written in the form am + bn and the number of positive integers that cannot be expressed in the form am + bn

HeronsLaw.py:
Calculates the area of a triangle based on its side lengths using Heron's Law.

RectsInGrid.py:
Counts the number of possible rectangles in a grid.

Palindrome.py:
Checks if input is a palindrome or not. Relatively useless by itself, but the concept can be applied to complicated problems.

ModularExponentation.py:
Calculates x^y mod z. Works for HUGE exponents.
